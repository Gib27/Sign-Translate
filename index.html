Sign Translate


About Us

Sign Translate would solve the problem of accessibility, communication, make a more inclusive and aware environment for the deaf community, and make learning sign language more accessible.

Stakeholders

The Deaf and Hard of Hearing Community

This community is often left out of society due to lack of knowledge of sign language. Creating an easily accessible application for people to communicate would create a more inclusive society for the deaf and hard of hearing.

Service Industry Workers

Many service industry workers interact with a lot of people with different types of abilities. Being able to learn sign language easily will equip service workers with the ability to communicate with the deaf and hard of hearing community.

People Interested in Learning Sign Language

Learning sign language is a good skill to learn to be more inclusive of the deaf and hard of hearing community. This application would make learning sign language easier and more accessible.
