Statistics

  • 28 million Americans (10% of the population) have some degree of hearing loss
  • 2 million Americans are classified as deaf
  • 10% of these 2 million (200,000) are born deaf
  • ASL is the most used sign language in the world
  • ASL is the 3rd most used language in the United States after English and Spanish
