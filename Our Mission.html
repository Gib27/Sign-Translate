Our Mission







Communication Is Key

Sign Translate aims to solve the problem of accessibility, communication, make a more inclusive and aware environment for the deaf community, and make learning sign language more accessible. With Sign Translate we aim to provide a platform that will enable non-ASL users to communicate with the Deaf community and those with any type of hearing loss. With 10% of the U.S. population having some sort of hearing loss, which is around 28 million people, and 2 million of those people being classified as deaf. The need for learning sign language is much needed for proper communication with the millions of people that are a part of the Deaf community. Sign Translate will make the world a more united place, one sign at a time.
