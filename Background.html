What is Sign Language?

Sign Language is a visual language that is often used by the deaf and hard or hearing community in order to communicate. Sign Language not only utilizes hand signs, but also body language (such as raising eyebrows when asking a question), reading lips, and showing facial expressions.

American Sign Language
